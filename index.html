FDM Printing

High-quality prints in a variety of materials, including PLA, ABS, ASA, PET-G, and TPU. Specialized options such as Carbon Fiber PLA and Carbon Fiber ABS for enhanced strength and durability. Whether you need flexible, durable, or high-strength prints, we have the right material for your project.

3D CAD

We offer 3D CAD services for designs up to medium complexity and assemblies with up to five components. Our team can create detailed and precise CAD models tailored to your specifications, which will be provided in .stl format for seamless integration with your 3D printing projects.
